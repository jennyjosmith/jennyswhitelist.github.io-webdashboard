    • Member Satisfaction

    When we cross-reference these findings (satisfaction with membership and plans to renew) with whether respondents believed their Chamber representative was exceptional at delivering value, we found 96% of those who strongly disagree/disagree that they are highly satisfied with their membership also strongly disagree/disagree that their liaison is exceptional at demonstrating value of the membership. Even about ¼ of those who are highly satisfied don’t find their liaison is exceptional at demonstrating value. There is opprtunity to strengthen proactive member engagement efforts to positively impact retention.
